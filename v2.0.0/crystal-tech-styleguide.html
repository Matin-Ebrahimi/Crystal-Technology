GOLDEN RULES

  • One accent only. Teal on ~5–10% of the screen. Never a second accent color.
  • Commit to dark. No light or cream backgrounds, ever.
  • No light-blue gradients. The only glow allowed is a faint teal halo behind the hero.
  • Strong size contrast. Big headings, calm body. Depth from borders + soft shadow, not color.
  • No accent lines under titles. Use whitespace instead.
